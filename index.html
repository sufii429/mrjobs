PharmaRep Jobs is Pakistan's premier job board dedicated exclusively to connecting talented Medical Representatives with top pharmaceutical companies. We specialize in opportunities for sales professionals in the healthcare sector across all major cities in Pakistan.

Why Pursue a Career as a Medical Representative?

Medical Representatives play a crucial role in Pakistan's healthcare ecosystem. As a pharma sales professional, you'll:

  • Build relationships with doctors, pharmacists, and healthcare providers
  • Promote life-saving medications and healthcare products
  • Enjoy competitive salaries with performance-based incentives
  • Receive continuous training on new products and medical knowledge
  • Develop transferable sales and communication skills
  • Have opportunities for career advancement into management roles

Qualifications for Medical Representative Positions

While requirements vary by company, most pharmaceutical firms in Pakistan look for:

  • Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy, Biology, or related field
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Valid driver's license and own transportation
  • Knowledge of medical terminology and human anatomy
  • Strong sales aptitude and negotiation skills
  • Ability to build relationships with healthcare professionals
  • Understanding of pharmaceutical industry regulations
